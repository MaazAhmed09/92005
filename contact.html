What you can start doing right now:

  • Conserve energy -

    Switch to energy-efficient lightbulbs, turn off lights when leaving a room, unplug electronics when not in use, and lower the use of thermostat or air conditioning.

  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle -

    Minimize waste by choosing reusable items, reducing consumption of single-use plastics, and properly recycling.

  • Conserve Water -

    Take shorter showers, fix leaky faucets, and be mindful of water usage in the home.

  • Raise Awareness -

    Educate friends, family, and classmates about climate change and its impacts.
