Purpose of the website

This website has been specificly designed to promote the idea of saving the environment from Climate Change. Climate change is a reality in New Zealand, with significant impacts already being observed and further changes projected for the future. This website will show the evidence of climate change, how our activities are contributing to climate change and how climate change affects New Zealand. It also provides details on what you can start doing right now to help save the environemnt from climate change.
