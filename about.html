What's hapennning?

New Zealand is experiencing and will continue to experience significant impacts from climate change, including rising temperatures, altered rainfall patterns, and sea-level rise. These changes will lead to more extreme weather events like droughts and floods, affecting various sectors like agriculture, ecosystems, and coastal communities.

What are you doing that affects climate change?

Teenagers contribute to climate change through their everyday actions, similar to adults, but also possess unique avenues for action. These include energy consumption (like using electronics and transport), waste generation, and dietary choices, all of which have an impact on greenhouse gas emissions and resource depletion.

Statistics

New Zealand is experiencing a warming trend, with increases in average temperatures, more frequent warm days, and a decrease in frost days. These changes are consistent with global trends and are linked to human-caused greenhouse gas emissions.
